Deep-Sea Creatures

Scientists have discovered organisms that survive in extreme pressure, darkness, and cold temperatures. These creatures often have unique adaptations, such as bioluminescence, which allows them to produce light in the deep ocean.

Hydrothermal Vents

Hydrothermal vents are underwater geysers that release hot, mineral-rich water. These environments support entire ecosystems that do not rely on sunlight, changing our understanding of how life can exist.

Seafloor Mapping

Advances in sonar technology have allowed scientists to map the ocean floor in greater detail than ever before. These maps reveal underwater mountains, valleys, and trenches that were previously unknown.
