Welcome

The ocean covers more than 70% of Earth's surface, yet over 80% of it remains unexplored. Ocean exploration is essential for understanding our planet, discovering new life forms, and addressing global challenges such as climate change and resource management.

By studying the ocean, scientists gain insight into ecosystems that support life on Earth. From regulating weather patterns to producing oxygen, the ocean plays a critical role in maintaining the balance of our environment.
