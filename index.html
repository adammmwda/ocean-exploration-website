Why Ocean Exploration Matters

The ocean covers over 70% of Earth, yet most of it remains unexplored. It regulates climate, produces oxygen, and supports millions of species.

Major Discoveries

  • New deep-sea species are found every year
  • Hydrothermal vents with extreme life forms
  • Hidden underwater ecosystems

The Future of Exploration

Advanced submarines, AI robots, and sonar mapping are helping scientists explore deeper than ever before.
